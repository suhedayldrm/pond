# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-02-03

This Privacy Policy explains how **German Vocab** (“the App”) handles user data.

## Overview
German Vocab is a German vocabulary quiz app. The App does not require user accounts and does not collect personal data.

## Information We Collect
### Personal Data
The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or identifiers.

### App Data Stored Locally
The App may store the following data **locally on your device only**:
- Quiz progress and scores
- Selected language level or preferences

This data never leaves your device and is removed when the App is uninstalled.

## Permissions
### Text-to-Speech
The App uses your device’s text-to-speech functionality to pronounce German words.
- No audio is recorded
- No microphone access is used

## Internet Usage
The App does not require an internet connection during normal use. Vocabulary data is bundled within the App.

## Third-Party Services
The App does **not** use:
- Analytics services
- Advertising SDKs
- Crash reporting tools
- Login or authentication services

Platform services provided by Google Play, Android, or Expo may process limited technical information necessary for app distribution and operation, subject to their own privacy policies.

## Data Sources
Vocabulary content is sourced from **DWDS (Digitales Wörterbuch der deutschen Sprache)**.
All linguistic data is used for educational purposes and attributed accordingly.

## Children’s Privacy
The App is not specifically directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Choices
You can remove all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the App.
